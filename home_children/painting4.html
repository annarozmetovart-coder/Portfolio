Ballerina Study

Year: 2022 Medium: Oil Painting on Canvas Size: 60" × 60"

This painting examines gesture, posture, and emotional expression through the figure of a ballerina at rest. The elongated pose and delicate arm positioning emphasize fragility and discipline. Muted background tones contrast with the lighter costume, directing attention to the form and movement of the body. The work focuses on capturing grace through structure, using controlled brushwork and subtle value shifts.
