Embrace

Year: 2025 Medium: Plaster Size: approx. 35 × 5.5 × 5.5 in

A simplified figurative sculpture exploring form, balance, and physical connection. The piece emphasizes smooth surfaces and rounded volumes, focusing on gesture and the relationship between two abstracted figures rather than anatomical detail.
