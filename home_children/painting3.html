Bear in the Forest

Year: 2022 Medium: Oil Painting on Canvas Size: 60" × 60"

This study explores natural light within a forest environment. The bear serves as both focal point and structural anchor within the composition. Layered brushstrokes and varied tonal values create depth in the trees and surrounding foliage. Reflections in the water introduce balance and symmetry, while soft diffused light enhances the realism of the woodland atmosphere
