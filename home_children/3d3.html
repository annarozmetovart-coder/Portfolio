Quiet Within

Year: 2025 Medium: Clay Size: 11.5" × 8.5" × 9"

A sculptural form exploring peace of mind through softened facial features and organic textures. The work reflects a moment of stillness, where emotional tension is held gently rather than resolved, suggesting calm as a state of acceptance rather than perfection
