MY WORK EXPLORES EMOTION AND MEMORY THROUGH QUIET, ATMOSPHERIC IMAGERY. I AM DRAWN TO MOMENTS THAT ARE OFTEN OVERLOOKED — FRAGMENTS OF FEELINGS, GESTURES, AND SPACES THAT HOLD TRACES OF PERSONAL EXPERIENCE.

I AIM TO CREATE VISUAL SITUATIONS THAT INVITE THE VIEWER TO SENSE AN EMOTION FOR THEMSELVES. I OFTEN USE NATURE, LIGHT, AND THE HUMAN FIGURE AS SYMBOLS OF INNER STATES, ALLOWING THE SUBJECT AND ENVIRONMENT TO MERGE EMOTIONALLY.

FOR ME, MAKING ART IS A WAY TO UNDERSTAND HOW MEMORIES SHAPE OUR PERCEPTION OF THE PRESENT. THROUGH THE PROCESS OF DRAWING AND COMPOSITION, I TRY TO TRANSLATE INVISIBLE FEELINGS INTO SOMETHING VISIBLE AND QUIET — SOMETHING THAT SPEAKS THROUGH STILLNESS RATHER THAN EXPLANATION.
